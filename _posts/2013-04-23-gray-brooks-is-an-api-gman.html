Gray Brooks has served at the Federal Communications Commission the last three years as part of the digital team overhauling the agency’s web presence and standing up their current Drupal site.In the lead-up to the release of the Digital Government Strategy, he transitioned to the General Services Administration (GSA) to serve as Senior API Strategist.As part of the agency’s recently launched Innovation Center, his role is to promote the role of web services in government and to support federal agencies in their implementation of the Strategy’s milestones.API Economist: Back in May 2012, the White House launched their Digital Strategy: Building A 21st Century Platform To Better Serve The American People.One of the key tenets of that strategy was to make open data content and web APIs the foundation of the platform.
