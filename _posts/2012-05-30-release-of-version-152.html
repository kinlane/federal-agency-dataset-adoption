Most of the indicators in the HIW have national level data.Many indicators also have data available by state, county, and hospital referral regions.The HIW contains indicators derived from and in support of several state and federal health indicator initiatives.County Health Rankings Community Health Status Indicators Healthy People 2020 The HIW provides access to the underlying data through the use of an Application Programming Interface (API) which is designed to present information to systems with disparate architectures and underlying technologies.
