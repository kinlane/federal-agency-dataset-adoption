Hurricane season officially began on June 1 and will last through November 30.While recovery efforts from Superstorm Sandy are still underway, and we grapple with devastating tornadoes too, we must also prepare for the possibility of more hurricanes.And unfortunately, NOAA is forecasting an “active or extremely active” season this year.A principal focus of the Public Safety and Homeland Security Bureau, which also draws on expertise from across the FCC, is to promote the reliability and resiliency of the nation’s communications infrastructure in times of disaster.Communications services are vital to keeping us safe, informed and connected to our loved ones.
