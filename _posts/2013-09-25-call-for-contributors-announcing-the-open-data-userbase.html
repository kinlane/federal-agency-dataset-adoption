Open data fuels many thousands of products and services.However, unlike the branding on laptops that forces owners to rest their hands on “Intel Inside” placards, most retail solutions don’t advertise that they use open data.This is generally a great thing because the license requirements encourage the widest possible use.Unfortunately, it also means that most people – even those active within the technology innovation ecosystem – don’t have a clear idea of how open data is actually being used.To address this problem, Socrata is launching a new crowd-sourcing project: the Open Data Userbase.
