By Jacob Fenton May 28 2013 10:27 a.m.Thanks to a loophole in federal election law that allows last-minute campaign spending to go undisclosed for months, an Arizona nonprofit was able to hide $4 million it spent during last years election -- 40 percent of its expenditures on campaign 2012 -- until earlier this year.At the end of last year, Americans For Responsible Leadership had disclosed spending about $5.4 million on federal races, the vast majority of it either bashing President Barack Obama or lauding his Republican opponent, Mitt Romney.
