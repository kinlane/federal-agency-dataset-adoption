The following guest post is by Chris Martin, researcher at the University of Leeds.Here he presents the results of his recent research into perceptions of the barriers to open government data.He looks at the similarities and differences between public and non-profit sector perceptions, pointing to the utility of greater cross-sectoral collaboration.Earlier in the year, as part of my on-going research on open data, I ran a survey exploring the barriers to open government data.I was seeking to understand how the open data community perceived potential barriers to realizing value from open government data.
