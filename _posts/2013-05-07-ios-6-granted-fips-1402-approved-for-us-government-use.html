iOS 6 granted FIPS 140-2 certification.(Image: CNET)Apples iOS 6 mobile operating system is now secure enough for low-level work in the U.S.government after it passed security certification.The National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST), which examines and tests mobile devices for security and validation purposes, granted the Apple mobile platform FIPS 140-2 certification (Level 1) last Friday.
