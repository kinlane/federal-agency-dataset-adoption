In the UK, the Prime Minister leads the government with the support of the Cabinet and ministers.You can find out who runs government and how government is run, as well as learning about the history of government.The Prime Minister is head of the UK government.He is ultimately responsible for all policy and decisions.He: oversees the operation of the Civil Service and government agencies appoints members of the government is the principal government figure in the House of Commons The Prime Minister is David Cameron MP and he is based at Number 10 Downing Street in London.
