Data.medicare.gov is the Center for Medicare and Medicaid Services’ (CMS) flagship portal for sharing valuable data about health and healthcare in the US.The site is a key component of CMS’s efforts to align with the Digital Government Strategy and make valuable data open, accessible, and machine-readable by third parties, and as part of its broader open data efforts to improve health and health care.Socrata worked with CMS to redesign data.
