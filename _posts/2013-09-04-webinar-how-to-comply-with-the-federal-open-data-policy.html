Through Executive Order 13642 and OMB Memorandum M-13-13, the White House has decreed that open and machine-readable are the new default settings for government data.Known collectively as the Federal Open Data Policy, these documents require federal government entities to transition to open data by November 1, 2013—less than two months away.What does the Federal Open Data Policy require?For an outline of the policy’s requirements and how Socrata can help you meet them, read our overview on Open Data Policy Compliance.Whether you’re looking for ways to enhance your existing open data platform or deciding on a good place to start, attending the webinar can help.
